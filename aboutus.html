Our story

We are a team of multimedia design students, united by a shared passion for making a difference through digital solutions. For our assignment, we focused on the Global Plastics Treaty, creating a website to support this vital cause. Our goal is to raise awareness, encourage participation, and provide a platform for future petitions that advocate for global action against plastic pollution. We're dedicated to making a lasting impact.

Who We Are

As multimedia design students, we specialize in web development, user experience, and digital communication. Our project focuses on the Global Plastics Treaty, aiming to bring attention to the issue of plastic pollution. Through our website, we hope to not only support this important petition but also create a space for future petitions tackling global challenges. We're committed to helping create a more sustainable and environmentally conscious world.
